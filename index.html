How to Update Windows

Objective

To guide you through the process of checking for and installing the latest updates for your Windows 10 or 11 operating system.

Prerequisites

  • A stable internet connection.
  • Device connected to a power source.
  1. Open the Windows Start menu. This is the button in the bottom-left corner of your screen shaped like the Windows logo.
  2. Type "Settings" in the search bar and click on the Settings app to open it.
  1. In the Settings menu, click on Windows Update (usually at the bottom of the left-hand menu).
  2. Click the Check for updates button.
  3. Wait for Windows to find, download, and install any available updates. Your computer may need to restart.
